My Journey

My love affair with technology began at age 9, when I first discovered the enchanting world of computers. That childhood fascination evolved into a lifelong passion that has guided me through over 25 years in software development.

My professional journey began in the United States, where I spent 7 years developing C++ cross-platform applications, honing my skills in creating efficient, portable software solutions. I then transitioned to Java applications, expanding my expertise in enterprise software development for several years.

Then, for over a decade, I poured my creativity into Android development, crafting elegant solutions and pushing the boundaries of mobile experiences. When that flame began to dim, I ventured into backend cloud services, gathering valuable knowledge but still searching for that lost spark.

Today, I've rediscovered that original magic in AI and Generative AI integration. This emerging field has rekindled my enthusiasm and given new purpose to my craft. I'm bringing my decades of experience and unwavering commitment to clean code into this exciting new chapter.
